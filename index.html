🌍 The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Education
Artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming the landscape of education worldwide. Personalized learning systems, powered by sophisticated algorithms, can now adapt to individual students' needs, pace, and learning styles. According to a recent study by the World Economic Forum, AI-driven educational tools have shown a 30% improvement in student engagement compared to traditional methods.

However, critics argue that over-reliance on AI may diminish the role of human teachers, who provide emotional support and mentorship that machines cannot replicate. The challenge lies in finding a balanced approach that leverages technology while preserving the human element in education.

📝 听力原文
Welcome to the university library orientation. I'm Sarah, the head librarian, and I'll be showing you around today.

First, let me tell you about our opening hours. During term time, we're open from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM on weekdays, and 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekends. During vacation periods, we close earlier at 5:00 PM every day.

Now, about borrowing. Undergraduate students can borrow up to 12 books at a time, while postgraduates have a limit of 20 books. The standard loan period is three weeks, but you can renew online if no one else has reserved the book.
💡 答案解析
题型分析：这是典型的 Section 1 场景——校园介绍。

考点提示：
• 时间/数字类信息会重复一遍，注意抓取
• "up to" 是限定词，答案前的修饰语不要漏掉
• 注意区分 undergraduate 和 postgraduate 的不同要求

高频场景词：orientation, loan period, renew, reserve, overdue
